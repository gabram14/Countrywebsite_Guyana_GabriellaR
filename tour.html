Here are a few beautiful Tour Attractions in Guyana:

Kaieteur Falls:

Kaieteur Falls is one of the most powerful single-drop waterfalls in the world. It is located on the Potaro River in Kaieteur National Park, central Essequibo Territory, Guyana. It is 226 metres high when measured from its plunge over a sandstone and conglomerate cliff to the first break.


Guyana Demerara Harbor Bridge:

The first 6,074-foot long floating toll bridge that crosses the Demerara River, joining Peter's Hall to Schoon Ord, Essequibo Islands-West Demerara in south america . It was commissioned on 2 July 1978. And ended but rebuilt with the named “Bharrat Jagdeo Demerara River Bridge”, as in early September 2025.

St. George's Cathedral:

:St. George's Cathedral is an Anglican cathedral in Georgetown, Guyana. The church is of Gothic Revival timber construction and reaches a height of 43.5 metres. It is the seat of the Bishop of Guyana. St. George's was designed by Sir Arthur Blomfield and opened on 24 August 1892. The building was completed in 1899.

Guyana Kingston Seawall:

:the Sea Wall is a 280-mile seawall that runs along much of Guyana's coastline, including all of the coastline in the capital city of Georgetown. It protects settlements in the coastal areas of Guyana, most of which are below sea level at high tide.
